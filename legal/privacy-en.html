Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Nihon.go is a Japanese learning app for studying Japanese words, readings, meanings, example sentences, review, and AI learning. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the app.

Information Stored

The app mainly stores the following information on the user's device:

  • Learning history, correct and incorrect answer history, mastered words
  • Notebook, my list, and imported word data
  • Display language, notification settings, and character icon settings
  • AI learning profile such as goals, interests, and common use situations
  • Groq API key entered by the user
  • Premium purchase status
  • Translation reports voluntarily submitted through the report feature (word, reading, level, language, current and suggested translations, and comments)

The app does not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, precise location data, or other information that directly identifies users as a core app feature.

Use of Information

Stored information is used to provide learning history, review, notebook, display language, notifications, AI learning, purchase status, ad-free premium status, and app improvements.

On-Device Storage

Most learning data and settings are stored on the user's device. The API key is stored using a secure storage area on the device. Data may be lost if the device is changed, damaged, reset, or if the app is uninstalled.

External Services

If the user sets an API key and uses AI features, prompts, search terms, learning goals, and AI profile information may be sent to Groq API. This information is handled according to Groq's terms and privacy policy.

The free plan may display ads. Google AdMob or other ad services may collect or use advertising identifiers, device information, and ad display information.

Premium purchases are processed by the App Store or Google Play. The developer does not directly collect credit card numbers or payment information.

Supabase (Cloud Storage)

Translation reports submitted through the report feature are stored in the cloud service provided by Supabase Inc. No personally identifiable information is included. Submitted data is handled in accordance with Supabase's privacy policy. Supabase Privacy Policy

Third-Party Sharing

The app does not sell personal information. Information may be sent to external services only as needed for AI features, advertising, in-app purchases, or legal compliance.

Notifications

The app sends learning reminder notifications only when the user allows notifications. Notifications can be changed or disabled in device settings.

Data Deletion

Users can delete locally stored data through app features, device settings, or by uninstalling the app. Information sent to external services is handled according to each service's policies.

Children

If minors use the app, they should do so with consent or supervision from a parent or guardian.

Changes

This policy may be updated as needed. Important changes may be announced in the app or by another appropriate method.
