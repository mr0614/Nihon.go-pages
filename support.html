Common Questions

  • The AI quiz feature is available when you set your own Groq API key in the app.
  • Learning history, missed words, My List, and imported words are mainly stored on your device.
  • Premium purchase processing is handled by the App Store.
  • If you delete the app, locally saved study data may also be deleted.
  • If you find an incorrect translation, please use the report feature in the app or contact support.
